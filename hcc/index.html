Quality Beans

We brew our handcrafted artisinal coffee from the freshest beans available from equal opportunity employers in Columbia, Peru, and Hawaii. We pick the finest quality bean in small batch markets to caffeinate even the weariest of dungeoneer. New brews are fashioned every week as per our onsite chalkboard bean schedule, and weekly raffles are offered to our patrons to name each new brew. Winners of each raffle get a free 1lb bag of beans!

Our Community

Founded in the heart of Brooklyn, 1992

Giving back

We strive to nourish and embolden the community that frequents our cafe. Hidden Crumb Cafe is proud to host fundraisers at our numerous events, and a percentage of our sales is donated to aid locals in need. Whether its helping adventurers find their party, or offering a short rest away from the rain, we aim to mutually support our community in the best ways possible.

Events

Every week our Game Masters organize many different types of game nights in order to satiate even the most reclusive adventurers. Here at the Hidden Crumb Cafe our campaigns are open to all walks of life and we work hard to provide a safe space to support positive gaming relationships. Feel free to ask any of our Game Masters about signing up for nightly game sessions. Our space is also available to host a party or private gathering (perfect for that final campaign session!). Admission is 5$ per gamer and comes with a free medium beverage of choice.
